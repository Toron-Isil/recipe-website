Tabulla

INGREDIENTS

  • 500 Grams of parsley
  • 1 cucumbers
  • 1 Tomato
  • 4 green onion
  • Rough bulgur 1/3 cup
  • green mint 1/4 cup
  • olive oil 1 cup
  • Lemon juice 4 cups
  • White vinegar
  • Salt
  • black pepper
  • lettuce
  • cabbage leaves

Preparation

  1. cut the parsley
  2. Cut the tomato, onions and cucumbers into very small pieces
  3. Put the bulgur In warm water for 30 minutes
  4. add mint, olive oil, Lemon juice, vineger, salt, and pepper then mix them.
  5. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before serving
  6. add some lettuce or cabbage leaves and serve
